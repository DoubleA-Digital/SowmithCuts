The challenge

Rosario's had been a fixture in Simi Valley for 15+ years, but their website was stuck in 2010. No mobile support, no online reservations, and buried on Google. Diners were finding competitors first.

What we built

We redesigned from scratch — a full-screen hero, a clean digital menu organized by course, and an integrated reservation widget that feeds directly into their front-of-house system. We also set up their Google Business Profile and implemented local SEO schema.

The result

Within 60 days of launch, online reservations tripled. They rank #1 in local search for their category. The owner called it the best investment they've made in years.
