About us

Two builders.

We started Double-A Digital because we got tired of seeing great local businesses get ignored online. So we decided to fix that.

Our story

Built in Simi Valley.
For Simi Valley.

Aarush and Ajay grew up watching the businesses that shaped this community struggle to compete online. Not because their products or services were bad — they weren't — but because they had outdated websites, no online presence, or were paying too much for too little.

So they teamed up and built Double-A Digital with a simple promise: show you a real demo before you spend a dollar.

Every site is built by hand — no page builders, no outsourcing. When you work with Double-A Digital, you work directly with the people who write the code.
